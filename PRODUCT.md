# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Indonesian families, groups of friends, and couples searching for villa rentals in Batu, Malang. They browse primarily on mobile phones, comparing options quickly. They want to see real photos, clear pricing (weekday vs weekend), and book instantly via WhatsApp.

## Product Purpose

Apamurahbanget is a villa rental catalog for the Batu, Malang region. It helps guests discover, browse, and book villas quickly with transparent pricing and direct WhatsApp contact. Success means a guest finds a villa they love, sees great photos, understands the price, and messages to book — all in under 2 minutes.

## Brand Personality

Warm, trustworthy, approachable. "Your local villa expert." Not a cold marketplace — feels like a friendly recommendation from someone who knows the area well.

## Anti-references

- Airbnb-clone aesthetic (too corporate, too white)
- Booking.com density (too much information, overwhelming)
- Generic Indonesian property listing sites (low-trust, dated)

## Design Principles

1. **Photos first** — Real villa photos are the product. They must be front and center, large, and beautiful.
2. **Mobile-native** — Most users are on phones. Every interaction must feel natural on touch screens.
3. **Transparent pricing** — Weekday and weekend prices shown clearly. No hidden costs, no confusion.
4. **One tap to book** — WhatsApp CTA always visible and prominent.
5. **Speed and trust** — Fast to load, easy to read, feels professionally curated.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Large touch targets (min 44px). High contrast text. Images with alt text. Reduced motion respected.
